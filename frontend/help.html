Frequently Asked Questions

1. Who can donate blood?

Anyone aged 18-65, weighing at least 50 kg, and in good health can donate blood.

2. How often can I donate blood?

You can donate whole blood every 8 weeks, or 56 days.

3. What should I do before donating blood?

Make sure to eat a healthy meal and drink plenty of water before your donation.

4. Is donating blood safe?

Yes, donating blood is a safe process. All equipment is sterile and used only once.

5. How is my blood used?

Your blood can be used for patients undergoing surgery, cancer treatment, or trauma care.

Instructions for Donors

Please ensure you meet the eligibility criteria before coming to donate. Bring a valid ID and arrive at the donation center on time. After donating, rest for a few minutes and enjoy some refreshments provided.
